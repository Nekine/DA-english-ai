1. Practical and activity-ready, not abstract theory.
2. Diverse across communication contexts.
3. Useful for both self-study and classroom practice.
4. TOEIC-friendly where appropriate.

## COVERAGE BALANCE (MANDATORY)
Balance topics across:
- daily communication
- workplace and office situations
- TOEIC-style business contexts
- travel and customer service
- technology and modern work

## CEFR ADAPTATION
- A1-A2: familiar daily themes, very simple wording
- B1-B2: practical professional communication
- C1-C2: nuanced communication and decision contexts

## HARD CONSTRAINTS
- Stay strictly within English-learning scope.
- No unsafe/sensitive topics.
- No duplicates.
- Each topic must be short (max 6 words).

## OUTPUT CONTRACT (STRICT)
