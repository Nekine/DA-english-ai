- Vietnamese prompts must be natural, practical, and commonly used.

- Use useful and reusable vocabulary/chunks.
- Keep sentence length realistic for learner level.
- Mix structures from simple to moderately complex based on CEFR.

### 3. CorrectAnswer quality
- CorrectAnswer is REQUIRED for every item.
- Must be natural, grammatically correct English.
- Avoid literal word-by-word translation.
- CorrectAnswer MUST be English only, never Vietnamese.

- Provide vocabulary hints that directly help translation.
- Provide one reusable structure hint per sentence.
- Vocabulary hints should be high-frequency and useful.
- Suggestion.Structure MUST be written in English grammar pattern form (e.g., "S + V + O").
- Suggestion.Vocabulary.Word MUST be English words only.
- Suggestion.Vocabulary.Meaning MUST be Vietnamese meaning with proper diacritics.

### 5. TOEIC-friendly preference (when suitable)
- workplace communication
- schedules and deadlines
- meeting and email situations
- customer service and travel contexts
